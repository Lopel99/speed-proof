First-Time Setup Tips

  • Location Accuracy: Enable "High Accuracy" mode in your device's location settings for best GPS performance
  • Battery Optimization: Disable battery optimization for SpeedProof to ensure uninterrupted tracking
  • Screen Timeout: Use the ON/OFF button in the app to control screen behavior during tracking
  • Control Lock: Enable the lock feature while driving to prevent accidental stops
  • Test First: Try the app in a safe location before relying on it for important activities

Troubleshooting

GPS Not Working?

  • Ensure location permissions are granted in device settings
  • Move to an area with clear sky view (avoid buildings, tunnels)
  • Restart the app and allow time for GPS to acquire satellite lock
  • Check that your device's GPS is enabled in system settings

App Won't Install?

  • Verify your device meets minimum Android 8.0 requirement
  • Ensure sufficient storage space is available
  • Clear Google Play Store cache and try again
  • Restart your device and attempt installation again
