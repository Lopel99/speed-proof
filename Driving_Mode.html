Driving Mode Overview

Speed-Proofs Driving Mode is designed for everyday drivers who want reliable, GPS-free speed tracking. Unlike traditional navigation apps that depend on satellites, Driving Mode uses forensic-grade sensor fusion to calculate speed, heading, and location with unmatched accuracy. This makes it ideal for commuters, long-distance travelers, and anyone who needs trustworthy data even in tunnels, urban canyons, or areas with poor GPS coverage.

Why the 5-Mile Backtrack Matters

The backtrack feature is one of the most important tools in Driving Mode. It continuously records your route and allows you to retrace up to five miles of your journey. This is especially useful if you encounter detours, miss a turn, or need to return to a starting point quickly. By combining timestamped records with compass-based navigation, Speed-Proof ensures that your data is not only accurate but also defensible in civic or legal contexts.
