?? Tracking Continuity

What Keeps Tracking Active:

  • App Session: Tracking continues as long as the app is running and not force-closed or paused
  • Location Permission: GPS access must remain enabled
  • Control Lock: Prevents accidental stops from screen taps or pocket presses
  • Signal Integrity: GPS may temporarily lose lock in tunnels or dense forests, but resumes once signal returns

Potential Interruptions:

  • Battery Depletion: Device runs out of power or enters deep sleep mode
  • GPS Signal Loss: Extended time in areas without GPS coverage
  • Manual Stop: User intentionally ends the tracking session
  • App Closure: Force-closing the app or system crash
  • Permission Revoked: Location access disabled mid-session
