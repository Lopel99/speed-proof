What is SpeedProof?

SpeedProof is a GPS-based safety application designed for both drivers and outdoor enthusiasts. Unlike traditional solutions that require expensive devices or multiple subscriptions, SpeedProof works entirely on your existing smartphone.

Whether you're commuting to work, taking a road trip, hiking a mountain trail, or exploring the backcountry, SpeedProof gives you the tools you need to stay informed and in control.
