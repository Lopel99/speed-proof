Complete Flight Data in Airplane Mode

SkyTracker works perfectly in Airplane Mode with GPS enabled, providing all these essential flight metrics:

Latitude & Longitude

Precise GPS coordinates of your current location

Altitude

Your height above sea level in feet or meters

Speed

Ground speed in mph or km/h calculated by GPS

Bearing

Direction of travel relative to true north

Accuracy

GPS signal quality and position precision

How It Works

GPS receivers work independently of cellular networks. When you enable Airplane Mode then manually turn on GPS, you get all location data while saving battery and complying with airline regulations.

Pro Tip: Maximize Battery Life

Enable Airplane Mode, then manually re-enable GPS. This gives you all the tracking functionality while dramatically extending your battery life during long flights.
