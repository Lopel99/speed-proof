Speed-Proof Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 18, 2025 | Author: David Hall

1. Introduction

Speed-Proof is a mobile application designed to help users monitor their speed, route, and environmental conditions during various activities?including walking, jogging, running, cycling, bike riding, and boating. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the limitations of the app?s accuracy and intended use.

By using Speed-Proof, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. What We Collect

a. Location Data

We collect GPS-based location data to calculate speed, route history, and timestamps. This data is only gathered during active tracking sessions and is stored locally on your device.

b. Weather Data

Speed-Proof integrates weather information from third-party APIs based on your location. This includes temperature, humidity, wind speed, and visibility.

c. Device Information

We may collect non-personal device data such as model type, operating system version, app version, and network status to improve app performance.

d. Usage Data

Anonymous usage metrics such as session duration, feature interactions, and crash reports may be collected to help us improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Display real-time speed and route information
  • Generate personal activity reports
  • Provide weather context for your sessions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Accuracy Disclaimer

Speed-Proof uses smartphone GPS and third-party weather services to estimate speed, location, and environmental conditions. While the app may closely match your vehicle?s speedometer or activity pace under normal conditions, it does not guarantee 100% accuracy in speed tracking or weather data.

Speed-Proof is designed for personal awareness and activity improvement?not for legal defense. It is not certified or intended for use as legal evidence in traffic disputes, insurance claims, or court proceedings.

GPS signals can be affected by terrain, signal interference, device settings, and environmental factors. All data generated by Speed-Proof should be treated as informational, not definitive or legally binding.

5. Data Storage and Control

  • Location and session data are stored locally on your device
  • You can delete your data at any time via the app settings
  • You may disable location tracking through your device?s permissions
  • We do not retain personally identifiable information on our servers

6. Who Should Use Speed-Proof

  • Walking, jogging, and running
  • Cycling and bike riding
  • Boating and recreational travel
  • Personal driving awareness
  • Route tracking for fitness or safety

The app is not intended for use by minors without parental supervision.

7. Legal Compliance

Speed-Proof complies with applicable privacy laws and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. We reserve the right to update this policy as needed to reflect changes in technology, law, or app functionality.
